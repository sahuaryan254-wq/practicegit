Hill Views & Nature

Gauridant Hill

Famous hill with beautiful sunrise and sunset views. Perfect for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

45 km from city Best time: 5-7 AM, 6-8 PM Entry: Free

Gadhpahra Hill

Historical hill with ancient ruins and panoramic city views. Great for trekking and historical exploration.

8 km from city Open: 6 AM - 6 PM Entry: Free

Sunset Point

Perfect spot to watch the sunset over the city. Near Chakraghat, offers breathtaking evening views.

Near Chakraghat Best time: 6-7 PM Entry: Free

Hill View Tips

Trekking

Weather
